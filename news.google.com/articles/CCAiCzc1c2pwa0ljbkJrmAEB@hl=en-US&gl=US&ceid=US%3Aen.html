Published on May 22, 2020

In this episode of On the Records, Matty Healy combs through The 1975's entire discography, album-by-album, and gets into what inspired his records. Matty breaks down everything encompassing his albums including where the records got their titles, the album art and much more.

Albums featured:
The 1975
I Like It When You Sleep, for You Are So Beautiful Yet So Unaware of It
A Brief Inquiry into Online Relationships
Notes on a Conditional Form
