Published on May 21, 2020

“Full House” star Lori Loughlin and her husband Massimo Gianulli have agreed to plead guilty in the college admissions scandal that broke last year. The couple will be sentenced to a combined seven months behind bars, plus hundreds of hours of community service, and up to $400,000 in fines. The couple paid $500,000 to get their daughters into USC. “Dance Moms” star Abby Lee Miller served eight months for banking fraud, and she told us in an exclusive interview that she thought Loughlin got off easy.
