Brill Corona Update

As the COVID-19 virus continues to spread, our thoughts go out to anyone who has been infected or impacted. We hope that you, your family, friends and colleagues are safe and well, and are taking the necessary precautions to keep it that way.

All Brill offices have taken the necessary measures to remain fully operational. We are complying with government directions in each location. Many of our staff members are working from home. If you need our assistance, please contact your regular Brill contact.
