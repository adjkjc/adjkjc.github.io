This Mourner’s Kaddish Is Set to Adele’s ‘Hello’ And It’s Perfect

By Joanna C. Valente Feb 9, 2016
Share Tweet

We’ve all lost someone important to us, and it’s one of the hardest, yet most universal, experiences in life. Of course, the best thing we can do is support each other and never let anyone deal with loss alone. And naturally, that’s the entire point of reciting kaddish–it forces mourners to join others.
