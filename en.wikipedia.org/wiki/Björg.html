Björg

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

Björg or Bjørg is a feminine given name of Old Norse origin in use in Scandinavian countries, including Sweden, Norway and Iceland meaning "help, salvation."[1] It is a popular middle name for girls born in Iceland.[2]
