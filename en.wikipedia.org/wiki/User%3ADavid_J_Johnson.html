David J Johnson is retired and lives in Dorset, UK.

Married to Susie for 53 years.

Formerly Senior Sales Manager at De La Rue and Harrison and Sons Ltd, banknote and security printers.

Charter Associate of the SETI Institute, Mountain View, California, USA

Large range of interests: including SETI, cosmology, archaeology, politics (UK and US), serial killers, missing persons, 9/11 Attacks and films.
