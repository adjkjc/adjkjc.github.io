Apophis, Apep or Apepi, (from Ancient Egyptian ˤ3pp, meaning 'ship') may refer to:

Ancient Egypt[edit]

  • Apep, an Ancient Egyptian mythological chaotic being
  • Apepi (pharaoh) (reigned c. 1580 – 1550 BC), a 15th-Dynasty Hyksos pharaoh
  • 'Apepi (fl. c. 1650 BC), a Lower Egyptian ruler hypothesised to be the king's son Apophis

Other[edit]

  • 99942 Apophis, a near-Earth asteroid
  • APEP F.C., a Cypriot football club.
  • Apophis (Stargate), a Stargate SG-1 character based on the demon
  • 2XMM J160050.7–514245, named Apep by its discoverers, a binary star system
