[1]Imran (also transliterated as Emran, Emraan, Omran, Umran, Emrah (Turkish) or Amram (in the Bible) Arabic: عمران‎) is an Arabic male name. In Turkish, Emrah can also be a given name for females. (The name Imran is a Quranic: House of ʿImrān (āl ʿimrān))
