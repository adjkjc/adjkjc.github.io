Ersa

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search
For other uses, see Ersa (disambiguation).

In Greek mythology, Ersa /ˈɜːrsə/ or Herse /ˈhɜːrsiː/ (Ancient Greek: Ἔρσα Érsa, Ἕρση Hérsē, literally "dew") is the goddess of dew and the daughter of Zeus and the Moon (Selene), sister of Pandia and half-sister to Endymion's 50 daughters.[1]
