Eastern Association of Rowing Colleges

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia
Jump to navigation Jump to search

The Eastern Association of Rowing Colleges (EARC) is a college athletic conference of eighteen men's college rowing crews. It is an affiliate of the Eastern College Athletic Conference (ECAC).[1]

Members[edit]

Eighteen colleges and universities are members of the EARC, mostly in the North-East and Mid-Atlantic United States, but also in the Mid-West with the University of Wisconsin–Madison.[2]
