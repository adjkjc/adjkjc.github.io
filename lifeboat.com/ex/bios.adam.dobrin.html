Print bio

Adam Dobrin

Adam Dobrin is Director delivering Direction at Ministry of Forbidden Knowledge and President at ERMC Global Services.
 
The Ministry of Forbidden Knowledge (MOFK) is dedicated to preserving Democracy and Freedom in the great United States of America. This is not your father’s USA, and its imperative that citizens are unwavering in their position that the Constitution is the supreme law of the land. Free speech, religion, and privacy are values that Americans are not willing to give up… if they could only remember.
 
ERMC provides consulting, outsourcing, and placement services to small and medium sized business related to information technology and employer risk. Services range from PEO/ASO program placement, to “pay as you go” business lines programs — often providing potential clients with significant savings over their current workers’ compensation and general liability premiums. Other services include tax credit solutions, I-9 and hiring practice automation, and w/c premium, classification, and claims assistance. If you own or operate a business involved in manufacturing, transportation, construction, or medical industries; chances are they can save you a significant amount of aggravation.
 
Adam was previously Partner at Viasensa Corp, IT Administrator at Emplify, Owner at EA ClamCo, Consultant at Oasis Outsourcing, WC Manager / IS at Certified HR Services, and Information Analyst at Cura Group.
 
Adam earned his Bachelor of Applied Science (B.A.Sc.) in Artificial Intelligence at Florida Atlantic University in 2006.
