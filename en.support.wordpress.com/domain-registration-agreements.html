Domains, Upgrades

Domains »Domain Registration Agreements

When you register a new domain on WordPress.com or transfer an existing domain to WordPress.com, you are presented with and agree to a domain registration agreement. If you didn’t make a note of it, don’t worry! You can look it up at any time at WordPress.com.

To determine which registrar agreement applies to a domain you’ve registered:

  1. Go to your Manage Purchases page.
  2. Select the relevant domain.
  3. Click Domain Registration Agreement. You’ll be directed to the relevant domain registration agreement, which will be one of the following:
  • Automattic Domain Name Registration Agreement (Automattic is the registrar of record)
  • Key-Systems GmbH Registration Agreement (Key-Systems is the registrar of record)
  • This Domain Name Registration and Customer Service Agreement (Wild West Domains is the registrar of record)
  • Tucows Domain Inc. Registration Agreement (Tucows is the registrar of record)

If you’ve opted for private domain registration, please refer to the table below to determine which privacy/proxy service terms apply to you.

Registrar Privacy/Proxy Service Terms
Automattic  Knock Knock WHOIS Not There Terms of Service
Key-Systems  Knock Knock WHOIS Not There Terms of Service
Wild West Domains  Domains By Proxy Agreement
Tucows  “Whois Privacy Service” section of the Tucows Domain Inc. Registration Agreement

WHOIS information for domains with Automattic as the registrar of record can be found at whois.sawbuck.com.
