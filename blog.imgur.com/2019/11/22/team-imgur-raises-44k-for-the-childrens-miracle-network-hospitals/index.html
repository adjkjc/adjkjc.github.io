Team Imgur Raises $44k for the Children’s Miracle Network Hospitals

November 22, 2019 Community, News

Sarah

November 2, 2019 was Extra Life Game Day – the time where all the Extra Life teams around the world do their 24-hour gaming marathons to raise money for sick and injured children at the CMN Hospitals. 175 Imgurians made up Team Imgur, our biggest Extra Life team to date, and we were on a mission to raise $30,000. Thanks to the support from our glorious community, we managed to raise $44,000 and counting, putting Team Imgur in the top 20 fundraising teams this year!

At Imgur HQ, we hosted 50 Imgurians from the Bay Area and beyond for an epic 24 hours of streaming and gaming. Words can’t describe the honor it was to have some of you fly out to be with us and join this incredible cause.

Streaming for 24 hours was a blast. @Pugabyte donated $50 for @BottlesOfBeer to put all of his hair gel in his hair at once. This was a lot more disgusting than one would imagine. #ForTheKids, though.
