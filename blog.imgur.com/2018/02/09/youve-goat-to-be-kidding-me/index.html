You’ve Goat to be Kidding Me

February 9, 2018 Community, Featured Images

Sarah

When most of us see a cute goat post, like this one from Imgurian Thelmplication, we giggle, upvote and go about our day, feeling that our spirits have been suitably lifted.

Baaaaa hahaha

When Imgurian Catscannotfly saw this goat post, they commented about how much they wished they had a goat. They probably weren’t expecting the OP to make this offer in reply.

Catscannotfly made their own post, imploring the community for upvotes on the original post to ensure it got over 10,000 points and they might get their promised goat.

Imugr please help me get a pet goat.

TheImplication appeared in the comments, potentially believing they had made a baa-aa-aa-d deal.

When you wake up and realise you need to start shopping for a goat…

Somethings goat to give! After waiting for what felt like an age, the community got their update.

Update on the free goat.

The two new goat friends decided that “Instead of getting a goat just for the sake of it we did the next best thing and just chose to care for one in a sanctuary. A goat bought would have been cute but it wouldn’t be fair for them this is the next best thing.” We approve of this decision.

Meet Dingle.

He’s a handsome fella and Catscannotfly couldn’t be happier.

All started with the post so thank you everyone who helped. Honestly I’m really surprised but now I have a goat called Dingle so can’t complain. Thanks as well to @TheImplication for delivering and now co-owning a goat with me.

There we have it, folks. Imgur is bringing people and goats closer together and we think that’s ruddy marvelous.
