Strong's Exhaustive Concordance
Aven

The same as 'aven; idolatry; Aven, the contemptuous synonym of three places, one in Coele-Syria, one in Egypt (On), and one in Palestine (Bethel) -- Aven. See also 'Own, Beyth 'Aven.
