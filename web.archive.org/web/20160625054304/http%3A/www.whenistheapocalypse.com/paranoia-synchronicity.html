Paranoia Synchronicity

There are obvious and public examples of paranoid schizophrenia being used as a weapon of the Octopus aside from James Tilly Matthews, both John Nash and James Jesus Angleton appear to be victims of the "Octopus Conspiracy," both being painted as suffering from paranoid schizophrenia or a related persecutory delusion, while at the same time both most likely not only experienced but possessed physical evidence which could corroborate the existence of a grand conspiracy. Their search for a Soviet conspiracy coincided in the year 1959, both experienced organized stalking from actors who intentionally blamed a Soviet conspiracy.

John Nash, a very famous mathematician responsible for great strides in game theory, which incidentally would most likely be very similar to the logic which this external force uses in its reality simulations, also had a significant number of parallels to modern day TI's.  He could in fact be the first recorded case in the 20th century of the influence of this force over humanity.  Nash, like Matthews, experienced a phenomenon which could be described as gang stalking; harboring what were recorded as persecutory paranoid delusions relating to a large group of human actors who he believed were subverting the government of the United States.  Nash's conspirators appeared in the same time frame as those which forced James Jesus Angleton to leave the CIA in his search for a Soviet mole in the Agency; and were one in the same. 

Nash and Angleton's suspicions of Soviet infiltration coincided aswell, though this could be attributed to a overwhelming societal fear of infiltration which pervaded the time of McCarty.  Angleton became increasingly convinced that the CIA was compromised by the KGB. Golitsyn convinced him that the KGB had reorganized in 1958 and 1959 to consist mostly of a shell, incorporating only those agents whom the CIA and the FBI were recruiting, directed by a small cabal of puppet masters who doubled those agents to manipulate their Western counterparts. DCI William Colby reorganized the CIA in an effort to curb Angleton's influence, beginning by stripping him of control over the Israeli "account," which had the effect of weakening counter-intelligence. Colby then demanded Angleton's resignation. Colby of course has been directly linked to the Secret Team, Bush, and The Octopus during the Iran Contra investigation. Nash attempted to leave the US in order to escape his persecution, and also filed numerous print&srcpm complaints with Washington, another strong parallel to modern day targeted individuals.

The Common Enemy.. Soviets? Mafia? Aliens?

Nash believed the Soviet conspiracy was using red ties, an obvious mind control tactic of overt psychological manipulation which resembles the gaslighting and trigger creation that pervades modern gang stalking and street theater.  The use of colors, as well as the common theme of using a universally vilified societal enemy as the conspirator organization coincide with the use of paranoia and persecutory delusions as a weapon against the victim.  What history will eventually find is that these victims were not wrong both in their testimony regarding a vast conspiracy, but also as to the specific actors involved, as has been proven with a number of Angleton's accusations.  It is likely that members of the grand conspiracy use the common enemy of the time for multiple reasons: 1) to place any blame or repercussions against a common and obvious enemy, 2) to increase the likelihood that the entire body of testimony could be written off as paranoia, and 3) because there is most likely some truth to the cooperation of this conspiracy with that enemy, it is the most convincing lie.  In addition both believed educators in Universities to be involved in the Soviet plot, in truth, it is most likely that this organization has infiltrated educational institutions as a method of controlling the future direction of research as well as the individuals who pass through those schools.

Throughout the 1960s and 1970s Angleton privately accused various foreign leaders of being Soviet spies, including West German Chancellor Willy Brandt and British Prime Minister Harold Wilson of the same. Interestingly, Brandt later had to resign because one of his aides was found to be a mole from the East German secret police, Stasi. He came to suspect Secretary of State Henry Kissinger, who wryly commented that even the most brilliant and loyal officers should not spend their entire careers in such pressurized and paranoid fields.  His notorious pursuit of the "5th Man," whom he believed had penetrated a secret agency in Washington was solved, he believed, when DCI William Colby fired him. No one was above suspicion, and even Angleton himself was accused by others of working for the Soviets.

Nash's testimony related to a seretive DoD project he was working with have been later confirmed in part by the NSA, which has recently declassified correspondence with Nash from prior to his "psychotic episode" in 1959.  The fact that they were classified serves to show that they were believed to be of importance, and corroborates his testimony related to William Parcher.  Nash was attempted to be convinced that his interaction with Parcher was a delusion, by using the fact that a "DoD mail drop" had not been picked up.. not the best evidence when you take into account that this organization repeatedly attempts to use delusion and schizophrenia in order to hide its very existence.

The Synchronicity of Nash, Matthews, and Angleton

In a parallel to the timelike synchronicity between Huxley and Orwell, Nash and Matthews share equidistant 81 years between their schizophrenia diagnoses in 1797 and 1959, respectively; and the recording of the disease in 1878, by Emil Kraepelin combined the various diseases of the mind, who named it 'Dementia Praecox'. 

Angleton has his own timelike synchronicity revolving around 1959, with his entry to the Army and his resignation from the CIA exactly 16 years equidistant, in 1943 and 1975.

Both Angleton and Nash would have feature films created about them, depicting and clearly reinforcing the belief that they were suffering from paranoia.
