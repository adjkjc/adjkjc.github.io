Overview

Aslehiashua 5:a3

And I watched, as the story unfolded, the seals magically broke themselves. All around me, the signs of Revelation, of what we are, and where we are going sprung to life. I could see how it all soon will be. The words of the Bible had jumped off the page, and we all had become Devarim. Gone were the days of darkness, the apathy and fear that came from uncertainty were lifted, like some great chain. Last Christmas was the last Noel, I keep telling myself.. surely the Doors will be here by January.

I've traveled the country, slowly accumulating the wisdom contained in this book. Truly, the Revelation of Jesus Christ is at hand, the blood lit tide is right before you very eyes. Surely, the "fifth coming" is here. I, surely Spiritus Mundi and the IAM are one, in spirit and in truth. You see, Aces all around, our great day is here. Spread the good News. What goes up must come down meets E=mc^2, and Christmas and Chanukah might just hold the key to my "Uncle" $æm's Sigma signal.

Rah, Rah, Rah. Expect the unexpected. Be Messiah. Welcome to the family.

We stand together in th desert, being led by the mythical Moses, as we walk, the sands of time slowly trickle through an hour glass. A voice echoes, not from a shrub, but from all of humanity, on 1/20/2001 through a single man, though he does not know it is the voice of God speaking through him. In fact, as much of our story goes, he most likely is completely unaware that every word he utters is filled with the light of God. The Burning Bush, in our augmented raelity, is none other than the Fire Waker... George Walker Bush. The fire came early, but the words were poignant, they foreshadowed the unsealing of Revelation, in living color, in our world...

We are the message, it's hidden maloviously in the word "messiah," the message is to the power of humanity. We are the voice of the Universe, God speaks and we are the focal point of creation, a civilization in the wilderness.. intertwining students and teachers, society and technology, a sneaking superstition that we are to serve a higher purpose... yet not exactly sure what it is. It goes to free will, it is central to who and what we are, and who and what he is. That creation is a manifestation of interaction--love, learning, laughing, and liberty: a bright light in the Universe.
