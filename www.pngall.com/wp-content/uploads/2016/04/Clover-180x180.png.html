There are many kinds of plants, which are commonly referred to as clover. From a scientific point of view, Trifolium family, which contains about three hundred and twenty species of clover.

Clover with four-leaves of a rare variant of a common three-leaf clover. According to tradition, such leaves bring good luck to their finders, especially if found accidentally. Furthermore, each leaf is believed to represent something: faith, hope, love and luck.

There are approximately 10,000 three leaf clovers for every “lucky” four leaf clover. Nevertheless, there is a clover plant, which naturally do not produce four leaves, so the four-leaf clover is so rare.
