HOW TO BECOME A BEING OF 100% PURE BEING OF LIGHT.

  • Posted by JASON on September 6, 2010 at 11:43am in Spirituality In General
  • View Discussions
I realize that we may all experience something different as a result of following the steps, but I want to share with you some of the experiences I had immediately after following the steps to convert to 100 percent light. Here is what I noticed happening right after I followed the steps:



1. I felt vibrating and buzzing inside my body as more light was coming into it. This actually felt good. I also felt a bit tired and needed to take a nap everyday for several days as I was experiencing the internal vibrating and buzzing and integrating of light. However, I felt so much peace and bliss while laying down and dozing off each time. It felt absolutely wonderful!



2. A few old issues that had not been brought to the surface to be released previously came to the surface like a bomb exploding and then were released within a few minutes.



3. I felt foggy minded for a few minutes.



4. I felt a bit dizzy/vertigo from the integration of light coming into me.



5. I could sense the new entities of 100 percent light accessing my akashic records. I believe that they actually get imprinted with all of our memories in our akashic records so that we do not end up losing any of our memories during the process. (This is my interpretation of what I was sensing taking place anyways!).



6. My prior dreams while sleeping at night usually showed me premonitions of difficulties and challenges that were going to happen in the future in my life. My dreams now have actually changed to show me that there is a new guidance team helping me, and that they are working on improving my life. I have also been getting premonitions while sleeping that things are going to change in my life very soon and for the better. This is such a change from what used to be the case.



7. Although I still go through days of cleansing due to ascension, and there are still tough ascension days, the days are much less difficult than before. I have also experienced more waves of joy, love, bliss, peace, and positive energy in general after having followed the steps in this book.



8. I feel more energetic more often. At times I feel very exhilarated.



9. I have more faith than before and have a more positive outlook on life.



I could go on and on, but you get the idea of what I am trying to say. My life is improving.



The author of the book gives permission in the book to copy the information and pass it along to others as long as the author's name is mentioned and the publisher is mentioned. So, if you find that these steps help you in any way, please feel free to pass them along and give credit to the author and publisher.



If you decide to convert to 100 percent light, then read the steps out loud slowly, making sure that you read everything exactly as it is written. Read the steps with conviction. It is also recommended that for a few days after reading through these steps that you take a few walks everyday to help with the energy circulation, blood flow to your brain, etc. in order to help with the integration of light. Please read the steps as they are written only. Please DO NOT add any new age words or rituals to this (I did add words and rituals the first 2 times I did it, and it messed things up.). Simply read the steps as they are written, nothing more, nothing less.



Although you may feel changes taking place immediately, please allow at least two weeks for your new guide team to get on board and for changes to begin taking place in your life. Again, I cannot promise that this will work for everyone, but at least it is worth a try.



Here are the steps for you to read exactly as they are written:



1. Your Guide Team: “From the Light of God that I am, I hereby declare that my team of guides shall, as soon as any necessary transference can be made, be comprised only of members of 100 percent pure Light who have never been re-programmed by entities of the dark.”



2. Your Primary Guide: “From The Light of God that I am, I hereby declare the entity that is my Primary Guide shall be an entity of 100 percent pure Light that has never been re-programmed by entities of the dark, with such change, if necessary, to be made as soon as possible.”



3. Your Consciousness: “From the Light of God that I am, I hereby declare that my consciousness mix should be converted, if necessary, to a mix of 100 percent pure Light of entities that have never been re-programmed by those of the dark. I further declare that this conversion should take place as soon as possible, but preferably during evening hours as I sleep, and as gently as possible in order that I might continue my normal daily routines.



4. From the Light of God that I am, I call forth that all things, whether animate or inanimate, within or around me, my home, yard, or place of business, be immediately deactivated from use as “directional beacons” by those who are not of 100 percent pure Light. I further declare that when this has been accomplished, it shall be irrevocable and permanent.



5. From the Light of God that I am, I call forth that no energies, entities, or beings are to be allowed anywhere around me at any time that are not of 100 percent pure Light. I further declare this to be irrevocable and permanent.



6. From the Light of God that I am, be it known that I hereby cancel all contracts and/or agreements I have made with ANY entity, in any time frame or in any reality, that were not in my best interest or in the interest of the Light, or that were made with entities who were not of 100 percent pure Light. I further declare that the cancellation of all such contracts is to be irrevocable and permanent in all time frames and realities.





--------------------------------



It is a good idea that whenever you ask for guidance that you demand that what comes through be only of 100 percent pure Light.



Please give this at least 2 weeks or so for changes to start taking place.



Information was taken by permission from “Dear God! What's Happening To Us?”, written by Lynn Grabhorn and published by Hampton Roads Publishing Company.



May you all be blessed on your journey! ﻿
