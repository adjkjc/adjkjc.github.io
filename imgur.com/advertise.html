Introducing Promoted Posts:
Native Ads that millennials love

 
 
 

We're on a mission to solve the biggest problem in advertising: how do you make ads that people actually like? That's the thinking behind Promoted Posts.

Promoted Posts are a lot like regular Imgur posts: a full-page canvas that can include tons of animated or still images, lots of descriptive text, and links to your site.

The only difference is they're boosted into the Imgur stream where they'll be seen by the influential, taste-making internet culture enthusiasts who visit Imgur every day.

That means you have plenty of room to tell your story without having to be intrusive or interruptive.
