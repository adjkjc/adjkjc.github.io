Proto-Indo-European[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Athematic root present of the root *h₁es-.

Verb[edit]

*h₁ésti (imperfective)[1]

  1. to be

Descendants[edit]

    • Lithuanian: būti (esu, esi, etc. (except yra))
