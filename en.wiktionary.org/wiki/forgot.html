English[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Received Pronunciation) IPA(key): /fəˈɡɒt/
  • (General American) IPA(key): /fɚˈɡɑt/
  • Audio (US)
    		(file)
  • Rhymes: -ɒt

Verb[edit]

forgot

  1. simple past tense of forget
  2. (archaic) simple past tense and past participle of forget
    • c. 1610-11, William Shakespeare, The Tempest, Act II scene ii[1]:
      Prospero: Thou liest, malignant thing! Hast thou forgot / The foul witch Sycorax, who with age and envy / Was grown into a hoop? Hast thou forgot her?
