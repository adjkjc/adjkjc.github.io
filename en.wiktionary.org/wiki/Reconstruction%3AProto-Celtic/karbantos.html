Proto-Celtic[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Unknown: “The a-vocalism and the root-shape (*k...b(ʰ)) make it probable that we are dealing with a loanword from some non-IE source.”[1]

Noun[edit]

*karbantos m

  1. chariot, war chariot
