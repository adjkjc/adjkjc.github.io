English[edit]

Alternative forms[edit]

  • idlely (obsolete)

Etymology[edit]

idle +‎ -ly

Pronunciation[edit]

  • Audio (US)
    		(file)
  • IPA(key): /ˈaɪd(ə)lɪ/

Adverb[edit]

idly (comparative more idly, superlative most idly)

  1. In an idle manner.
  2. Without specific purpose, intent or effort.
    I idly played with the paper, not even realizing I was folding it into a paper airplane.

Anagrams[edit]

  • idyl, ylid
