Noun[edit]

指事

  1. (Chinese character classification) one of the types of Han characters such as 上 (shàng) above and 下 (xià) below that indicate an abstract idea with a non-arbitrary logogram

Noun[edit]

指事(しじ) • (shiji) 

  1. (Chinese character classification) ideogram, particularly in the sense of 六書 ideogram. One of the types of Han characters, containing those characters such as 上 above and 下 below that indicate an abstract idea with a non-arbitrary logogram.

See also[edit]

  • Category:Han ideograms
  • 六書 (りくしょ, ​rikusho): The six types of Han characters

    • 会意 (かいい, ​kaī): compound ideograph
    • 転注 (てんちゅう, ​tenchū): derivative cognate
    • 仮借 (かしゃ, ​kasha): phonetic loan
