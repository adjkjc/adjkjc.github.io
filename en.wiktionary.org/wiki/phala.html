Chichewa[edit]

Pronunciation[edit]

  • IPA(key): /ˈpʰa.ɽa/

Noun[edit]

phala class 5

  1. a kind of thin porridge

  • nsima

Noun[edit]

Etymology[edit]

Vedic Sanskrit फल (phala).

Noun[edit]

phala n

  1. fruit (of a tree)
  2. result, benefit
