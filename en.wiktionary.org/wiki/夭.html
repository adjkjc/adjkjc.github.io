Definitions[edit]

夭

  1. to die young, to die prematurely
  2. to be aggrieved
  3. to devastate
  4. disaster

References[edit]

  • (Min Nan) Taiwan Min Nan Common Words Dictionary (臺灣閩南語常用詞辭典)

Definitions[edit]

夭

  1. young, fresh-looking
  2. tender, gentle
  3. lush
  4. Alternative form of 妖 (yāo).
