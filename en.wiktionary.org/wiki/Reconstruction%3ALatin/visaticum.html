Latin[edit]

Etymology[edit]

vīsus +‎ -āticum

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (Classical) IPA(key): /wiːˈsaː.ti.kum/, [wiːˈsaː.t̪ɪ.kʊ̃ˑ]
  • (Ecclesiastical) IPA(key): /viˈsa.ti.kum/, [viˈzaː.t̪i.kum]
  • (Vulgar) IPA(key): /βiːˈsaː.ti.kum/, [biˈsa.de.ɡõ]

Noun[edit]

*vīsāticum n (genitive *vīsāticī); second declension

  1. (Gallo-Romance) face

Declension[edit]

Second declension neuter.
