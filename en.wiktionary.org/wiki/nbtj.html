Egyptian[edit]

English Wikipedia has an article on:
Two Ladies
Wikipedia

Etymology[edit]

nb +‎ -tj

Pronunciation[edit]

  • (modern Egyptological) IPA(key): /nɛbti/
    • Conventional anglicization: nebti

Noun[edit]

 f du

  1. dual of nbt (“mistress, lady”)

Proper noun[edit]

 f du

  1. The Two Ladies; the goddesses Wadjet and Nekhbet.
  2. Conventionally written before a king’s Two Ladies name.
