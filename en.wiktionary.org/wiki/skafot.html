Danish[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Middle Low German schavot, from French échafaud, from Italian catafalco (“catafalque”).

Noun[edit]

skafot n (singular definite skafottet, plural indefinite skafotter)

  1. scaffold (elevated platform on which a criminal is executed)
