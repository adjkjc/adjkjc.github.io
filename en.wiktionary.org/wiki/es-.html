Catalan[edit]

Etymology[edit]

From Latin ex. Doublet of ex-.

Prefix[edit]

es-

  1. indicates movement away or separation
    ‎es- + ‎fulla (“leaf”) → ‎esfullar (“to remove leaves from”)

Spanish[edit]

Prefix[edit]

es-

  1. indicates separation
    ‎es- + ‎coger → ‎escoger
  2. indicates removal or elimination
    ‎es- + ‎pulga → ‎espulgar
  3. indicates intensification
    ‎es- + ‎forzar → ‎esforzar
