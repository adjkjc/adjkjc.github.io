Blogging: Fundamentals

Get grounded in blogging basics.

Blogging Fundamentals is two weeks of bite-size blogging assignments that take you from “Blog?” to “Blog!”

Every day, you’ll get a new assignment to help you publish a post, customize your blog, or engage with the blogging community. Whether you’re just getting started or want to revive a dormant blog, we’ll help you build good blogging habits and blogger connections that will keep you going over the long haul.

Blogging: Commenting Basics

Overcome the fear of commenting.

This five-day challenge will get you out of your commenting comfort zone and help you make the connections with other bloggers that ultimately make blogging so rewarding. Each day, we’ll give you a discrete commenting task, along with our favorite tips on writing great comments and good commenting etiquette. The rest — where you comment, what you say, who you connect with — is up to you!

Blogging: Intermediate Customization

Making your site your own.

This ten-day course takes you in-depth into the free customization options available to WordPress.com bloggers. Understand all the ins and outs of your theme, create a totally unique custom header, coordinate and change your background and fonts, and learn to work with widgets, including custom image and text widgets. You’ll wrap up by learning some basic HTML, which gives you even finer control over the ways posts, pages, and widgets appear.

Launch your business' online presence.

Photography: Developing Your Eye I

View the world through your unique lens.

This 10-day course is a fun introduction to the fundamentals of photography. Each day, we’ll give you a theme to inspire your image, along with a related shooting tip. We’ll share advice on composition, image orientation, point of view, and color. Each assignment helps you sharpen your visual eye and encourages you to post daily on your blog. You can use any camera you like: the lens on your phone, a point-and-shoot, or a dSLR.

Photography: Developing Your Eye II

View the world through your unique lens.

Like Developing Your Eye I, this is a 10-day course for bloggers of all levels. Each day, we’ll give you a theme to inspire your image, along with a related photography tip. Hone your visual eye and experiment with light, motion, and surfaces; learn to observe your scene; and enhance your photos with image editing tools. You can use any camera you like: the lens on your phone, a point-and-shoot, or a dSLR. This course builds upon the assignments in Developing Your Eye I, but you do not need to take I before II.

Writing: Finding Everyday Inspiration

Find fresh inspiration in unexpected places.

Writing: Finding Everyday Inspiration is a twenty-day course that warms up your writing muscles and helps you find inspiration in the places closest to you — but where you might not think to look.

This course is designed especially for bloggers interested in personal writing, essay, and nonfiction — but everyone, from fiction writers to poets to others with niche blogs, will find new ways of brainstorming post ideas. You’ll get a new writing prompt each day to help you explore new formats, pull from your own experiences, and get familiar with online resources from publishing tools to social media.

Writing: Shaping Your Story

Look at your writing with an editor's eye.

Writing: Shaping Your Story is a course on the art of revision: four weeks dedicated to self-editing and rewriting. Go in-depth into the elements of focusing and building a story, whether fiction or nonfiction. Each week, you experiment with our techniques  and advice in your posts; what you actually publish, and how much, is entirely up to you. Ultimately, the goal is to learn to read your writing with an editor’s eye, and to improve one or multiple posts in your archives.
