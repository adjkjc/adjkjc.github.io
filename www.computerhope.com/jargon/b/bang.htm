Bang

Updated: 03/06/2020 by Computer Hope

Bang may refer to any of the following:

1. A bang is slang used to describe the exclamation mark.

2. Bang describes the physical process of hitting an object, often the keyboard. For example, a person may get frustrated with their computer and bang their hands on the keyboard.
