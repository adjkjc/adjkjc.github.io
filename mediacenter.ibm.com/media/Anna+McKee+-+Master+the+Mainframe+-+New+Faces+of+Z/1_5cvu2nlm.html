Anna McKee is the 23-year-old winner of Master the Mainframe in 2017 and 2018. She graduated from the University of North Texas in May 2019 with two degrees, a B.S. in Business Computer Information Systems and a B.B.A. in Decision Sciences. She currently works as a systems administrator for IBM on the Dallas Independent Software Vendors (ISV) team.
