    Yes, you can! You can use your existing domain. It’s easy to do. In the signup process you will have four options to add a domain. Select the option that says “I will use my existing domain and update my nameserver.”

    Add your domain here and complete the sign-up process. Next login to the account where your domain is hosted and update your domain nameserver. You must point it to us.
